Problem
The monotony of accounting processes leads to a loss of focus, leading to an increase in careless errors. As a result, routine operations take longer, reducing efficiency and creating unnecessary workload.
Solution
Our Mandarin AI service helps eliminate monotony by automating repetitive accounting tasks: data entry, document reconciliation, report generation, and error checking. It reduces the risk of accidental inaccuracies, speeds up workflow, and optimizes employees' time, allowing them to focus on what truly matters.

Why are We?
Reason for Choosing Us
We all have friends or family members who work as accountants, and they all told us almost identical stories. When we visited their offices, we'd see them spending hours creating spreadsheets, reports, and performing seemingly simple, yet incredibly tedious tasks. A few hours would pass, and we'd return, with the scene remaining virtually unchanged. It was this observation that spurred our team to do everything possible to solve this problem. Our motivation became boundless: eliminate routine, give accountants back their time, and allow them to work more easily and efficiently.
Road Map

Planning & Prototype Visualization

AI Training & Data Processing
